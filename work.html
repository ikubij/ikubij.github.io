Itrans

Web design/UI/MySQL

ITrans bus booking system is an online system that helps both passengers and bus companies by simplifying the travelling process. The passengers can check online for available vehicles to book, they can compare prices of different companies before booking. Companies have to register to the system and their available vehicles in operation. The system also generates tickets for the customers and enables online payment. It also has analysis for the companies’ module, where companies can see their earnings in a day, week or month by use of graphs.
